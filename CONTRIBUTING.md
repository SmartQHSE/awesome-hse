# Contributing to awesome-hse

Thanks for contributing! A curated list lives or dies by the quality of its entries.

## How to add a resource

1. Find the appropriate section (or propose a new one if none fits).
2. Add the entry in alphabetical order (or grouped by relevance where existing entries already follow a logical order).
3. Use the format: `- [Name](url) — One-line description of what it does and who it's for.`
4. Keep descriptions under 150 characters.
5. No promotional language. "The best" / "most powerful" / "revolutionary" will be rejected.

## What belongs here

- HSE / EHS / QHSE software and platforms
- Free tools, calculators, open datasets
- Regulations by country (primary regulator links preferred)
- International standards (ISO, IEC, ASTM)
- Industry bodies and professional associations
- Certifications and training providers
- Academic journals and peer-reviewed research
- Books (HSE, human factors, HRO, safety science)
- Podcasts, YouTube channels, newsletters, blogs (must be active in last 12 months)
- Open-source libraries
- Annual HSE conferences
- Other "awesome-X" lists that overlap

## What doesn't belong

- Marketing landing pages for paid courses with no curriculum published
- Personal consultancy websites (unless they publish significant free educational content)
- Products/services without clear HSE focus
- Dead links
- Affiliate links (will be scrubbed)

## Commit format

Conventional commits please:

- `add: ABC Platform to software section`
- `update: NEBOSH certification URL`
- `remove: XYZ (broken link)`
- `fix: typo in description of Sphera`
