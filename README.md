## Free tools & calculators

- [SmartQHSE HSE Calculators](https://tools.smartqhse.com) — 21 regulation-cited calculators: TRIR, LTIR, LTIFR, DART, EMR, WBGT, HAV, REBA, RULA, NIOSH lifting, risk matrix, fire load, evacuation, manhours, and more. [Open source](https://github.com/SmartQHSE/hse-calculators) (MIT).
- [OSHA $afety Pays](https://www.osha.gov/safetypays/estimator) — Estimate direct and indirect costs of workplace injuries.
- [NIOSH Lifting Equation Calculator](https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/ergonomics/nlecalc.html) — Official NIOSH calculator.
- [HSE UK Vibration Calculator](https://www.hse.gov.uk/vibration/hav/vibrationcalc.htm) — HAV / WBV exposure calculator.
- [ACGIH Heat Stress Calculator](https://www.acgih.org) — WBGT-based heat stress assessment.
- [CCOHS Heat Stress Calculator](https://www.ccohs.ca/oshanswers/phys_agents/heat/heat_health.html) — Canadian heat stress guidance.
- [OSHA Heat Illness Prevention](https://www.osha.gov/heat-exposure) — Heat index resources.

## Open datasets

- [SmartQHSE HSE Benchmarks 2026](https://www.smartqhse.com/datasets/hse-benchmarks-2026) — Consolidated TRIR/LTIFR/DART/fatality rates across 15 countries and 20+ industries. CC BY 4.0.

- [BLS Census of Fatal Occupational Injuries (CFOI)](https://www.bls.gov/iif/cfch.htm) — US fatal injury statistics.
- [HSE UK Statistics](https://www.hse.gov.uk/statistics/) — UK workplace injury and illness statistics.
- [IOGP Safety Data](https://www.iogp.org/safety-data/) — Annual upstream oil & gas safety performance report.

## Regulations by country

### United Kingdom

- [HSE UK](https://www.hse.gov.uk) — UK Health and Safety Executive.
- [RIDDOR 2013](https://www.hse.gov.uk/riddor/) — Reporting injuries, diseases and dangerous occurrences.
- [CDM 2015](https://www.hse.gov.uk/construction/cdm/2015/) — Construction (Design and Management) Regulations.

### European Union
